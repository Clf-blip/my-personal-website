About C-Soko

C-Soko is an innovative online marketplace tailored for students, making buying and selling seamless, secure, and hassle-free.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with a reliable and efficient platform to buy and sell second-hand goods, reducing waste and fostering sustainability.

Why Choose Us?

  • 🛡️ **Secure Transactions** - Built-in fraud prevention and verified sellers.
  • 👨‍🎓 **Student-Centric** - Exclusively designed for students to buy and sell easily.
  • 🚚 **Hassle-Free Delivery** - Integrated logistics for smooth transactions.
  • 📚 **Diverse Categories** - From textbooks to tech gadgets, find everything in one place.
